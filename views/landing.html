Memory Game

How to play


  • The objective is to find all pairs of cards in one minute.
  • To flip a card, look directly at it.
  • When 2 pairs of cards match, they remain facing up.
  • When 2 pairs of cards don't match, they flip back again to be facing down.
  • Hint: The trick is to remember which cards are where.

Match the shapes

How to play


  • The objective is to match each shape in the upper row with the one that corresponds to it in the lower row within one minute.
  • To select a shape, look directly at it.
  • When 2 selected shapes match, they remain selected with a green box surrounding them.
  • Hint: Each shape in the upper row has only one shape that corresponds to it in the lower row and vice versa.
